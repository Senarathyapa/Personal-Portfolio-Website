My Journey Into Software Quality

I'm Amanda Nadeeshani, an Information and Communication Technology undergraduates with a deep passion for software quality assurance. My QA journey began when I realized that great software isn't just about writing code, it's about ensuring every feature works flawlessly for the end user.
